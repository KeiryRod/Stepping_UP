Pronunciation

The only way to learn is by practicing. A conversation helps you to increase your vocabulary, also, to pronounce correctly each word.

Improvisation

Speaking is going to obligate you to improvise your answers. Therefore, you need to be fast for understand and for answering something correctly, even if you do not know the topic.

Fluency

Just practice helps you a lot, It allows you to find out trust in yourself, and the capacity to look for synonyms when you are speaking or listening another person.

Read a Text Aloud


You will be asked to read a piece of text aloud. You will be scored based on your pronunciation and intonation. Take a deep breath and try to read at a natural pace.

Describing a picture


You will be asked to describe a picture. You need to provide as many details about the picture as you can in the time allowed. Describe the basic picture and then break down the details in a logical manner.

Respond to Questions


You will be given a scenario and asked to answer three questions.
